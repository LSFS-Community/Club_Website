About Coding Club

Welcome to "Coding Club BMSIT&M" - your gateway to the tech universe! We're not just a coding club; we're a vibrant community of innovators and dreamers. Imagine building amazing projects, from cutting-edge web and app development to mind-blowing AI/ML and IoT creations, all while learning and growing together.
